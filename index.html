Stupické Polní Rané

Originating from Czechoslovakia it is one of the most hardy varieties, surviving until the first frost in the UK. The plants are potato leafed, produce a mixed yield of round and mini beefsteak red fruits that can be harvested in 55 days from planting out. Stupice tomato plants can make a lot of foliage due to their potato leaf nature and have grown up to 6ft in my borders (the best way to grow them is along a trellis as they produce more when not pruned.
